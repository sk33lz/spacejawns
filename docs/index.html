What is Space Jawns?

Space Jawns is a space game series currently in the planning and alpha phase of development. The first installment is a free to play 2.5D space shooter called Space Jawns Alpha. Space Jawns Alpha can be played on PC, Mac, Linux, and Android operating systems.
